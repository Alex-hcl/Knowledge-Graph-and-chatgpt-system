Random Sampling Strategy

After the completion of random sampling, the integration phase involves the utilization of graph neural network models, such as GraphSAGE, for aggregating neighbor information. The design of an intelligent strategy for this aggregation process is crucial to enhance the accuracy of the information presented to the user. ChatGPT, leveraging its comprehension of the user query context, plays a pivotal role in guiding GraphSAGE to select the most relevant neighboring nodes. This ensures that the aggregated information is not only comprehensive but also aligned with the specific context of the user's query.

These strategies work synergistically, enabling the system to adaptively aggregate neighbor information based on the specific characteristics of the user query and the underlying knowledge graph structure. The chosen strategy or a combination thereof can be tailored to meet the unique requirements of the application domain.
